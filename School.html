What is CIT?

Computer information technology (CIT) is the use and study of computers, networks, computer languages, and databases within an organization to solve real problems. The major prepares students for applications programming, networking, systems administration, and internet development.

What is the WDD?

Web development (WDD) is the process of creating websites and web applications. It involves a range of skills and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various web development frameworks.

What is CSE?

Computer science and engineering (CSE) is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves the design, development, and application of software and hardware solutions to solve complex problems.

What is ITM?

Information technology management (ITM) is the study of the use of information technology in business and organizational settings. It involves the planning, implementation, and management of IT systems to support business objectives.

What is Art?

Art (ART) is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting, drawing, or sculpture.

What is Comm?

Communication (COMM) is the process of exchanging information, ideas, and messages between individuals or groups. It involves both verbal and non-verbal methods of conveying information.

What is B?

Business (BUS) is the activity of making, buying, and selling goods and services. It involves the management of resources, the creation of value, and the generation of profit.
